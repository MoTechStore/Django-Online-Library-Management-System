Want to know about us ?

This is BookApp, Django web based online library system founded by Noel Moses Mwadende, founder & CEO of MotechApp YouTube channel and motechapp.com.The main aim of BookApp is to connect people who have home based personal library into one library so as to share different books from different countries across the globe. For that purpose, people can upload books they want to share but also they access shared books from other users.
